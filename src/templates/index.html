How it works

Robia Loans is a platform that connects approved loan seekers with verified providers. We make the process of getting a loan simple, transparent and efficient for everyone involved.

For loan seekers, we provide a quick and easy way to get a loan from multiple providers. You can compare bids and choose the one that best suits your needs. For providers, we offer access to a large pool of approved loan seekers, allowing you to grow your business and reach more customers.

Why choose Robia Loans?

  • Verified Providers: We thoroughly vet all our providers to ensure they are trustworthy and reliable.
  • Competitive Bids: Get multiple bids for your loan and choose the one that offers the best terms.
  • Transparent Process: We provide clear information about loan terms, fees and provider details, so you can make informed decisions.
  • Customer Support: Our dedicated support team is here to assist you every step of the way, ensuring a smooth experience.

Security

Why do we need this information?

We require this information to ensure the security and integrity of our platform. By verifying the details provided, we can protect both loan seekers and providers from fraudulent activities and maintain a safe environment for all users.

We only use this data for the verification process. Once you pass these checks we will, at your request, delete your files and future verification checks will require re-uploads of these files, if needed.

How long do we keep your information?

We use state of the art cryptographic tools to not only keep your data secure, but to make sure no one can identify you from it.
